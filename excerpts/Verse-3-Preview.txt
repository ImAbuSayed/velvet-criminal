Agg wangu sarhda main burning up high,
Tu hasdi ae jadon ni reaching the sky.
Kaliyan raatan vich tera sath milda sure,
Tere ishaareyan da nasha labhda cure.
Duniya di fikraan chhad ditti main behind,
Tu hi ae hamesha always on my mind.
Har aashiq nu kardi ae giving a test,
Par ishq mera baakiyan to aunda ae best.
