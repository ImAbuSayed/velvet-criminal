Kala surma jadon laavein tu looking so fine,
Dil dharkda mera when you cross the line.
Raat di chandni vich tera chehra karda glow,
Bina soche tere kol aawan moving real slow.
Tere hasse di aawaz kardi ae nasha magic spell,
Tu hi ae mere jeen da reason I can tell.
Ik ik adaa teri kardi ae strike,
Mainu apna bana lai doing what you like.
